COMPUTER ENGINEERING DEPARTMENT
ONLINE WEB-BASED COMPENDIUM SYSTEM

Online Web-Based Compendium System is a collection of thesis in both bachelor and masters degree under the Computer Engineering Department of Polytechnic University of the Philippines from year 2000 to 2019. This system aims to provide conveniency to students in which they can access this system if their thesis is already available or not. Students can also use these for references on their ongoing thesis including data collection, formats, etc. This system enables the user to enter a name of an author, co-authors and title. Upon entering the details needed the system will then display the Title, Author, Co-Author, Date Published and the Abstract of the study.
